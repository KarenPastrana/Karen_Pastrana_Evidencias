My Final Project

Taekwondo electronic breastplate

The idea of creating an electronic breastplate to be able to train in the sport of taekwondo is because it is not usually a cheap and easy to get equipment. The Universidad Ibero Puebla last year went to compete in a competition called CONADEIP, where several private institutions in Mexico participate, but because it is not usually train with this type of equipment, it was very difficult when trying to get the points because the sensors are only found at certain points, plus in the competition they ask everyone to bring their electronic bibs that besides being expensive, were out of stock. Here is a picture of my younger sister with the equipment on to start her combat.

So, the idea of my project is to create a breastplate that has the sensors and allows us to have a way to train more appropriate and thus get results of what we should focus on to get better results, as well as the interface to be able to visualize the results. Probably the sensors that would be used are pressure and bluetooth.


Research

Some of the problems of the current breastplate:

The problem lies in the fact that electronic breastplate are very sensitive and register very slight impacts such as a simple rub or even a strong wind push and mark them in the score.

They are heavy and present in all major taekwondo competitions. They do not have sensors all over the bib, so sometimes they do not mark a kick that clearly went in.

Advantages

Judges are impartial and there are fewer errors in the counting of points.
